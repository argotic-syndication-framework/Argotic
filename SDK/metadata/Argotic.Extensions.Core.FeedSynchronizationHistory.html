Remarks

Either or both of the When or By properties must be present; it is invalid to have neither. It is recommended that implementers specify both When and By properties whenever possible.

Implementations should not assume that the By property will be a human-readable indication of the author of the item; it is simply intended as a unique identifier for use in the synchronization algorithm. The precise entity represented by an endpoint will vary depending on the application.

Remarks
